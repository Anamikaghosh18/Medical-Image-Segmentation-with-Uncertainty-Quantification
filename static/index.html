How to read the heatmap colors:

Low Certainty (10%) Medium Certainty (50%) High Certainty (100%)
Red / Orange (70% - 100% certainty): Core Spot Area. The AI is highly confident this is part of the spot.
Yellow / Green (30% - 70% certainty): Borderline Zone. The edge or border where the AI is moderately sure.
Cyan / Blue (10% - 30% certainty): Low Certainty Area. High chance of being healthy skin, with low chance of belonging to the spot.
Uncolored (<10% certainty): Normal skin surface. The AI is confident this is completely standard healthy skin.

How to Understand Your Pathology Metrics

📏 Spot Size & Physical Diameter:

  • Under 6.0 mm: Typically standard for typical moles or healthy freckles.
  • Over 6.0 mm: Atypical size. Worth mention or monitoring by a doctor.

⚖️ Asymmetry Overlap Index:

  • Under 35%: Balanced/Symmetric shape. Highly common in benign features.
  • Over 35%: Irregular/Asymmetric growth. Worth standard clinical review.

🌟 Border Shape & AI Certainty:

  • Border Shape: Values near 1.00 denote standard circular shapes. Scores above 1.50 highlight highly jagged edges.
  • AI Certainty (Confidence): Represents how statistically sure the segmentation model is about the identified outline borders.
